About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Swansea University, supported by an EPSRC CASE Conversion and UK Atomic Energy Authority (UKAEA) scholarship. My research focuses on model calibration, optimal design, and data assimilation for digital twins of fusion reactor components, with an application to the UKAEA Chimera facility. With a background in Computational Mechanics, I have expertise in finite element modeling, numerical analysis, and simulation techniques. I am skilled in utilizing advanced tools, including Artificial Neural Networks and reduced order models, to solve complex engineering challenges. I am passionate about bridging theoretical concepts with practical applications to advance innovative engineering solutions..
